Email Filters¶

See the GitHub support page for how to filter certain types of email notifications. These are the most useful Cc addresses when reviewing in web-platform-tests:

  • review_requested@noreply.github.com when you are added as a (suggested) reviewer on a pull request.

  • assign@noreply.github.com when you are added as the assignee (i.e. as the reviewer) on a pull request.
